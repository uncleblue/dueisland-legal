Privacy Policy

DueIsland · Effective date: June 29, 2026

DueIsland (the “App”) is a menu-bar reminders and timers application for macOS, developed by Hornjoy (“we”, “us”). We respect your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data we collect

None. All reminders, timers, and settings you create are stored locally on your device. We do not operate a server and do not collect, transmit, or have access to your data. The App contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Purchases

The optional one-time “Full Version” unlock is processed by Apple through the App Store (StoreKit). Payment and account information are handled entirely by Apple; we never receive your payment details. The App only learns whether a purchase or restore succeeded, which is used solely to unlock features on your device.

Notifications

The App uses local notifications to alert you about your reminders. These are scheduled on your device and are not routed through any external server.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose any personal data, because we do not collect any.

Children

The App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy: fangbaiyu@gmail.com

隐私政策

DueIsland · 生效日期：2026 年 6 月 29 日

DueIsland（“本应用”）是由 Hornjoy（“我们”）开发的 macOS 菜单栏提醒与计时器应用。我们尊重你的隐私。本政策说明本应用如何处理你的信息。

我们收集的数据

无。你创建的所有提醒、计时器与设置均仅存储在你的设备本地。我们没有服务器，不收集、不传输、也无法访问你的数据。本应用不含任何第三方统计、广告或追踪 SDK。

购买

可选的一次性“完整版”解锁由 Apple 通过 App Store（StoreKit）处理。支付与账户信息完全由 Apple 处理,我们绝不接触你的支付信息。本应用仅获知购买或恢复是否成功,该信息仅用于在你的设备上解锁功能。

通知

本应用使用本地通知来提醒你。通知在你的设备上排程,不经过任何外部服务器。

数据共享

我们不出售、不共享、不披露任何个人数据——因为我们根本不收集。

儿童

本应用不面向儿童,也不会在知情的情况下收集任何人的信息。

变更

我们可能不时更新本政策。更新将发布于本页,并标注新的生效日期。
